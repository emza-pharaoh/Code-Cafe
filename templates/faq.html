Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: What is Code Café?

A: Code Café is a community for passionate developers to learn, collaborate, and share knowledge.

Q: How can I join Code Café?

A: You can join Code Café by registering on our website. Visit the registration page to create an account.

Account and Profile

Q: How can I reset my password?

A: You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page.

Q: Can I customize my profile?

A: Yes, you can customize your profile by visiting the "Edit Profile" page after signing in.
